Why Ada?

Inspired by the woman who
invented the future.

Ada Lovelace was the world's first computer programmer. In the 1800s, when women weren't supposed to do mathematics, she wrote the first algorithm ever designed for a machine.

She didn't just belong in STEM — she created it. AdaAscend exists to carry that spirit forward. Every woman who uses this platform is continuing the work Ada started.
